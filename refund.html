Refund Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Digital Products

Due to the instant-delivery nature of digital products, all sales are final once the product has been downloaded or accessed. We cannot verify whether a digital product has been copied or used after delivery.

Exceptions

We will gladly provide a full refund if:

- You were charged incorrectly or multiple times for the same product.
- You received a corrupted or unreadable file and we are unable to provide a working replacement.
- The product was significantly different from its description.

How to Request a Refund

Contact us at contact@treborify.com within 14 days of purchase with your order details and reason for the request. We aim to respond within 24-48 hours.

Our Commitment

We stand behind the quality of our products. If you are unsatisfied for any reason, reach out to us. We will work with you to make it right, whether through a replacement, credit, or refund at our discretion.
